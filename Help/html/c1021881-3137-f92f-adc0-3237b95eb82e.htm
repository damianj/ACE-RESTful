JointMoveCommand Properties

The MainWindowJointMoveCommand type exposes the following members.

Properties
  NameDescription
Accel
Maximum acceleration of the robot when it moves.
Decel
Maximum deceleration of the robot when it moves.
JointPosition
Specifies the joint positions of the move that is to be executed.
MotionEnd
Determines how the motion should finish (i.e., Blend, NoNull, SettleCoarse, or SettleFine)
SCurveProfile
Specifies the S-curve trajectory profile, from 0 (for trapezoidal) to 8, or -1 to leave unchanged. An S-curve can smooth the trajectory into an "S" shape by limiting the rate of change of acceleration. Robots with flexible links or drive trains can benefit from S-curves to reduce oscillation.
Speed
Maximum speed of the robot when it moves.
StraightMotion
Determines whether the robot's motion should be straight or not (i.e., true: straight-line, false: motion will be joint-interpolated)
